ITS CRANCH!

cranch lore

cranch was brought into this world long ago through the mind of some kind of jar jar binks and cher hybrid found at the depths of the cranch bogs

items needed to summon the cranch gods

  • 1 bottle of hidden valley ranch
  • at least a clam full of cranch berries
  • the blood of your sworn enemy(you can substitute this with cranberry sauce)
  • your cranch vessel

how to summon the cranch lord 101

  1. first you're gonna want to shake up the ranch real good while chanting the magic words. you know the ones
  2. after you've shaken your ranch vigorously you're gonna want to kiss your clam goodbye one last time
  3. toss the clam and cranch berries into the blood or sauce and chant more of the magic words
  4. you will now have to defeat the 2 headed sauce goblin (one head is gucci mane) with your cranch vessel
  5. after you have defeated the beast you must now scoop his remains and the rest of your ranch into the vessel
conclusion i think

if you have completed the steps above as shown then you should have a beautiful cranch to go with the pan fried chicken you made in the previous recipe.
